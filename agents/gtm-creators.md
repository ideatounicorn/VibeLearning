# Agent: Creator Partnership Playbook

5 micro-influencers, "30-day career switch challenge" format. Budget: $1,500–2,000 total or revenue share. Run in Month 2.

## Creator profile to target
- 20K–200K followers on TikTok or YouTube Shorts
- Content niche: career advice, job hunting, "day in my life as a PM/marketer", productivity, AI tools
- NOT: general tech YouTubers, coding channels, or lifestyle creators
- US-based audience (ask for audience geo before partnering)

## Where to find them
- TikTok search: `#careerswitch #productmanager #breakingintotech #careeradivce`
- YouTube search: "how I became a product manager", "career switch into tech"
- LinkedIn: search "career coach" + check if they post video content

## DM / outreach script
```
Hey [name] — I love your content on [specific video they made].

I run VibeLearning — a structured learning platform for people switching into tech. We're looking for 5 creators to do an honest "30-day career switch challenge" using our platform.

Here's the deal: you use VibeLearning for 30 days on whichever career path interests you, and you post your honest experience. We pay [flat fee $300–500] + 30% of every subscriber who uses your link.

No script, no fake enthusiasm. If you hate it, say so. We're confident you won't.

Interested? I'll send you a free Pro account to try first.
```

## Content brief (send to creators who say yes)
```
Format: 1–3 videos over 30 days documenting your journey
Video 1 (Day 1): "I'm switching careers in 30 days — here's my starting point"
Video 2 (Day 14): "Two weeks in — is this actually working?"
Video 3 (Day 30): "What I learned + honest review"

Requirements:
- Show the actual product (screen share or phone)
- Mention vibelearn.app and your discount code [CREATOR_CODE]
- No requirement to be positive — be honest

What we provide:
- Free Pro account
- Unique discount code (1-month free for their audience)
- $[flat fee] on delivery of all 3 videos
- 30% revenue share on subscribers who use their code
```

## Tracking
Create a unique Polar promo code per creator: `MARCUS30`, `JAMIE30`, etc.
Each code gives their audience 1 month free.
Track conversions per code in Polar dashboard.

## Priority creator niches (ranked by conversion likelihood)
1. "How I became a PM" creators — exact ICP
2. Career switch / resume coaches with video content
3. AI tools creators who also talk about career applications
4. "Study with me" / accountability creators

## What success looks like
- 1 video with 50K+ views → expect 500–1,500 profile visits, 50–150 signups
- 5 creators × average 20K views = 100K impressions, ~500 signups, ~25 conversions = $725 MRR

## Done when
- 5 creators contracted and briefed
- Each has a unique promo code in Polar
- UTM tracking set up for each creator's link
- First video published
